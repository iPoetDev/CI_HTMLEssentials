The Article Element

The article element is used for any self-contained sections within a web page, which can be independent of the other content. For example a news article, blog or forum post.

The aside element is used for a portion of a document where its content is indirectly related to the document's main content. The aside element is often used as a sidebar or call-out box.

HTML5 Semantic elements include the article, aside, header, nav, footer, and section elements.
